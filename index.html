About Us

Combining the strengths of the GeroWallet and bajuzki teams, we give you a 2,500-piece collection that, in our opinion, checks out all the boxes for unique, collectable, and utility-driven NFT collaboration. Featuring hand-drawn Mecha Stags with over 100 traits, the holders of this collection will be able to reap the benefits in both our ecosystems.

GeroWallet strives to be the ultimate Cardano wallet that boasts interchain and multi-chain compatibility – while providing a superior user experience through an intuitive UI. We are a team of developers, designers, blockchain enthusiasts, and IT professionals dedicated to bringing the next generation of banking solutions to the world.

Bajuzki studio is a group of designers, game developers, and artists who believe in the potential of blockchain technology. Over the last year, we have released multiple NFT collections - including comic books and characters featured in our upcoming game - and have been helping other projects in the Cardano blockchain space.
